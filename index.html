A Legacy of Freshness in Udaipur's Mandi

The name Jamiatmal Jagatram has been a cornerstone of Udaipur's New Sabzi Mandi for years. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between dedicated farmers and businesses seeking high-quality, fresh produce.

As a respected family business, we value the trust and relationships we've built over time. We proudly serve hotels, restaurants, caterers, and retailers across the region with a consistent supply of nature's best fruits and vegetables.

Our Diverse Range of Produce

Local Gems

Sourced directly from farms around Udaipur and Rajasthan, featuring seasonal favorites like mangoes, guavas, and fresh vegetables bursting with local flavor.

National Harvest

A curated selection of the best produce from across India, including crisp apples from Himachal, juicy oranges from Nagpur, and premium vegetables.

Imported Exotics

Delight your customers with a variety of premium imported fruits such as kiwis from New Zealand, dragon fruit from Southeast Asia, and berries from the Americas.
