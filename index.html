Supplying Freshness Across Rajasthan

Founded in the heart of Udaipur, Jamiatmal Jagatram has grown to become a leading wholesale supplier of fresh fruits. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between dedicated farmers and businesses seeking high-quality produce.

We are a family-run business that values integrity, strong relationships, and exceptional service. We proudly serve hotels, restaurants, caterers, and retailers across the region with a consistent supply of nature's best.

Our Diverse Range of Fruits

Local Gems

Sourced directly from farms around Udaipur and Rajasthan, featuring seasonal favorites like mangoes, guavas, and pomegranates, bursting with local flavor.

National Harvest

A curated selection of the best fruits from across India, including crisp apples from Himachal, juicy oranges from Nagpur, and sweet grapes from Maharashtra.
